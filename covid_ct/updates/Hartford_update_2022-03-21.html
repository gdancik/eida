EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (03/21/2022)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 03/18/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 459 (a decrease of 20.9%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 42 (a decrease of 12.5%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 25 (a decrease of 43.2%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
