EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (03/13/2022)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 03/11/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 2845 (an increase of 323.4%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 29 (a decrease of 17.1%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 52 (an increase of 2%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
