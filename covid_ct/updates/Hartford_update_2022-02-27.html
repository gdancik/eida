EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (02/27/2022)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 02/25/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 1242 (a decrease of 32.1%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 39 (a decrease of 29.1%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 81 (a decrease of 33.1%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
