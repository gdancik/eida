EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (12/07/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 12/03/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 4324 (a decrease of 3.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 57 (an increase of 39%)
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 315 (an increase of 43.2%)
