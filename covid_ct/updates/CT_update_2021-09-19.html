EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (09/20/2021)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 09/17/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 6660 (a decrease of 4.5%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 48 (a decrease of 15.8%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 332 (a decrease of 8.8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
