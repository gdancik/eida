EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/05/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/03/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 9826 (a decrease of 56.6%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 36 (an increase of 56.5%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 329 (a decrease of 13.4%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
