EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (12/28/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 12/23/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 4796 (a decrease of 36.7%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 84 (an increase of 10.5%)
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 389 (an increase of 2.1%)
