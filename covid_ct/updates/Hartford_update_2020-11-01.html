EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (11/02/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 10/29/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 1389 (an increase of 44.8%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 22 (an increase of 37.5%)
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 117 (an increase of 53.9%)
