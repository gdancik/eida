EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (10/19/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 10/15/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 3466 (an increase of 56.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 20 (an increase of 5.3%)
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 184 (an increase of 33.3%)
