EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (11/01/2021)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 10/29/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 3220 (a decrease of 39.9%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 43 (a decrease of 20.4%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 190 (a decrease of 15.9%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
