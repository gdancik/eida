EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (11/15/2021)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 11/12/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 3532 (an increase of 14.2%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 33 (a decrease of 10.8%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 225 (an increase of 4.7%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
