EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (10/11/2021)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 10/08/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 971 (a decrease of 17.6%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 11 (a decrease of 75.6%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 95 (an increase of 8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
