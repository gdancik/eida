EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (02/12/2022)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 02/11/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 2972 (a decrease of 30.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 47 (a decrease of 42%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 136 (a decrease of 51.6%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
