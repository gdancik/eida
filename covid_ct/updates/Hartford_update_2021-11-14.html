EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (11/15/2021)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 11/12/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 919 (an increase of 9.1%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 10 (a decrease of 16.7%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 66 (no change from last week)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
