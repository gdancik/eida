EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (12/06/2021)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 12/03/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 9543 (an increase of 19.6%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 72 (an increase of 63.6%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 420 (an increase of 48.9%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
