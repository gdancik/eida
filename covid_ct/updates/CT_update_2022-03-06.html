EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (03/06/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 03/04/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 3337 (a decrease of 52.9%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 105 (a decrease of 41%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 167 (a decrease of 34.5%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
