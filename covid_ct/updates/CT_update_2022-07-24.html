EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (07/24/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/24/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 5544 (a decrease of 5.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 19 (a decrease of 56.8%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 229 (a decrease of 15.8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
