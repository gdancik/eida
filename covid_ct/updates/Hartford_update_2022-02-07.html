EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (02/07/2022)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 02/04/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 3518 (a decrease of 60.5%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 59 (a decrease of 23.4%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 249 (a decrease of 42.8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
