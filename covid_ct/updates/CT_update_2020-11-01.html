EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (11/02/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 10/29/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 6336 (an increase of 81.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 49 (an increase of 40%)
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 329 (an increase of 54.5%)
