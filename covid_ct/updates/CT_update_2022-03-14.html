EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (03/14/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 03/11/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 5885 (an increase of 84.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 136 (a decrease of 1.4%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 140 (a decrease of 27.1%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
