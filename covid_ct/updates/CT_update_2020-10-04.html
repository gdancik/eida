EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (10/05/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 10/01/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 1982 (an increase of 39.6%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 16 (an increase of 33.3%)
