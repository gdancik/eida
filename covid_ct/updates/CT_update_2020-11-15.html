EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (11/16/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 11/12/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 13272 (an increase of 82.5%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 92 (an increase of 64.3%)
