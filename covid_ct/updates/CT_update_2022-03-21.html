EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (03/21/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 03/18/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 2805 (a decrease of 50.6%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 108 (a decrease of 22.3%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 92 (a decrease of 43.2%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
