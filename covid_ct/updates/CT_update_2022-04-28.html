EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (04/28/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 04/27/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 8359 (an increase of 12.8%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 25 (an increase of 38.9%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 210 (an increase of 44.8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
