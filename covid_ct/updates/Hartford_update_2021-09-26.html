EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (09/27/2021)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 09/24/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 1558 (a decrease of 4.7%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 22 (an increase of 4.8%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 90 (a decrease of 17.4%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
