EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (02/20/2022)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 02/18/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 1525 (a decrease of 41.9%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 22 (a decrease of 51.1%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 72 (a decrease of 36.3%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
