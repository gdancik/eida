EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (09/20/2021)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 09/17/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 1422 (a decrease of 19.3%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 10 (a decrease of 37.5%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 78 (a decrease of 24.3%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
