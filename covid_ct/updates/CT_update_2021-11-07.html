EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (11/08/2021)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 11/05/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 3253 (a decrease of 5.6%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 38 (an increase of 11.8%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 197 (an increase of 1.5%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
