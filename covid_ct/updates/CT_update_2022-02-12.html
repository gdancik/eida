EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (02/12/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 02/11/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 10458 (a decrease of 44.1%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 177 (a decrease of 42.7%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 467 (a decrease of 50.1%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
