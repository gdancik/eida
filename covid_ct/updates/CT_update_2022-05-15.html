EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/15/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 05/13/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 16311 (an increase of 75.1%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 56 (an increase of 133.3%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 323 (an increase of 31.8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
