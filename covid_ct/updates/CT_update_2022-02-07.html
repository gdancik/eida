EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (02/07/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 02/04/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 15790 (a decrease of 57.2%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 258 (a decrease of 25.6%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 785 (a decrease of 41.7%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
