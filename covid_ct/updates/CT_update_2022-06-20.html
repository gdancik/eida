EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/20/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/17/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 5885 (a decrease of 30.9%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 15 (a decrease of 74.6%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 261 (a decrease of 5.4%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
