EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/08/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 05/06/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 10201 (an increase of 22%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 36 (an increase of 44%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 239 (an increase of 13.8%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
