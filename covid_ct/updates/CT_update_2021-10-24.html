EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (10/25/2021)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 10/22/2021,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 4505 (a decrease of 3.2%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 34 (a decrease of 38.2%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 211 (a decrease of 13.9%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
