EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/23/2022)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 05/20/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 22515 (an increase of 52.8%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 43 (an increase of 34.4%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 354 (an increase of 18%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
