EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (03/07/2022)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 03/04/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 809 (a decrease of 28.4%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 15 (a decrease of 51.6%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 42 (a decrease of 17.6%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
