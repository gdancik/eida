EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (02/20/2022)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 02/18/2022,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 2949 (a decrease of 5%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 52 (a decrease of 11.9%)*
  • the number of hospitalizations at the end of the week was 73 (a decrease of 54.1%)

*Note that as of July 7, 2021, CT is updating COVID-related death counts once a week, on Thursdays.
