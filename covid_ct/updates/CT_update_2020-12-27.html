EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (12/28/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 12/23/2020,

  • the number of new cases reported last week was 17281 (a decrease of 37.7%)
  • the number of COVID-19 associated deaths reported last week was 325 (an increase of 1.6%)
